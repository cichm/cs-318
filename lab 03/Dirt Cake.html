Dirt Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 package of chocolate pudding mix
  • l bag of Oreos
  • 1 bag of gummy worms

Directions

  1. follow the directions on the pudding mix box to make the chocolate pudding.
  2. crush the Oreos into fine particles.
  3. add how many Oreos and gummy worms you want to your pudding.

Enjoy your Dirt Cake.
