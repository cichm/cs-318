Puppy Chow French Toast Dirt Cake

Puppy Chow

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Chocolate chips
  • 1 cup Peanut Butter
  • 1-2 cups Powdered Sugar
  • 6-7 cups Chex Cereal

Directions

  1. melt the chocolate and peanut butter together.
  2. add cereal to a large bowl. pour 1 cup of chocolate/peanut butter mixture over cereal.
  3. stir until cereal is coated.
  4. let mixture cool slightly.
  5. add powdered sugar. Mix until combined.

You are ready to enjoy your Puppy Chow!
