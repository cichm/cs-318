Puppy Chow French Toast Dirt Cake

French Toast

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • cinnamon

Directions

  1. mix all your ingredients besides the bread together.
  2. set a frying pan on your stove, and set the heat to medium.
  3. dip a slice of bread into the mixture and place on your frypan to cook.
  4. cook the bread on each side until it is golden brown in color.
  5. reapeat until all of the mixture is gone.

Enjoy your French Toast!
